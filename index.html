To find a park and its relevant information, first select either the State or Activities tab to show the respective drop-down menu. Select an item from one of the drop-down menu's and then click the search button to generate a list of parks (or sites) relative to your selection. Click on the park you are interested in to display an image, short description, and a list of activities available at the park, as well as the address of the location. Each park you visit will be stored in park history so you can re-visit it later.
