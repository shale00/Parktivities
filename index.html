To find a park and its relevant information, first select either the State or Activities tab to show the respective drop-down menu. Select an item from one of the drop-down menu's and then click the search button to generate a list of parks relative to your selection. Click on the park you are interested in, to display an image of the park and a list of activities available at that park. If you want explore other park options but are interested in the park you selected, click on the Save Park button to save that park for your reference.
